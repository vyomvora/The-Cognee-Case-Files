How to Play

  1. Click a place on the left to search it. Clues you find get pinned to the board.
  2. Click a person on the left, then pick a question. Their answer gets pinned too.
  3. If a red warning shows up, one person's answer does not match a clue you found. Open that person again and click Confront to call them out on it.
  4. Stuck? Click Hint for a nudge.
  5. When you think you know the answer, click Final Deduction. Drag the person you suspect and the three clues that back up your case into the boxes, then submit.

You only win if your answer is backed by real evidence -- naming the right person is not enough on its own.
